# aic Privacy Cockpit: The Unified AI Compliance & Governance Platform

**aic Privacy Cockpit** is a comprehensive, enterprise-grade governance ecosystem engineered specifically to automate AI risk management, data privacy conformance, and regulatory compliance for modern organizations.

By transforming static compliance matrices into active "Policy-as-Code" engines, aic Privacy Cockpit eliminates the agonizing overhead of human audits and evidence gathering.

---

## 🚀 1. Getting Started & Onboarding
*Accelerate your compliance journey from day one.*
- **Quick Setup Wizard (`/onboarding`)**: A dynamic 6-step wizard that automatically architects your initial compliant footprint based on your sector and size.
- **System Discovery (`/ai-inventory`)**: Automated tools to scan your infrastructure and detect AI models and data processing nodes.
- **Guided Onboarding**: Step-by-step assistance for new compliance officers to map their first regulatory framework.

## 🏠 2. Executive Command Center
- **Executive Dashboard (`/`)**: High-level visual telemetry representing overall compliance health, upcoming renewal bottlenecks, and real-time risk severity scoring.
- **AI Agent Insights (`/analytics/ai-insights`)**: Suggestive intelligence from our compliance agent to identify gaps and auto-generate corrective actions.
- **Global Intelligence**: Real-time regulatory feed from multi-jurisdiction monitoring.

## 🤖 3. AI Governance & Inventory
- **AI Systems Inventory (`/ai-systems`)**: A centralized ledger to document and manage registered AI systems, deployment states, and vendors.
- **Risk Classification (`/ai-assessment`)**: Automated tiering based on EU AI Act (High-Risk, Limited, Minimal) and DPDPA requirements.
- **Impact Assessments (`/impact-assessments`)**: Specialized AIA/DPIA forms that calculate risk profiles and auto-generate mitigation strategies.

## 🔒 4. Privacy & Data Protection
- **ROPA Management (`/processings`)**: A robust Record of Processing Activities tracking exactly how user data is utilized by AI models.
- **Data Subject Requests (`/dsr`)**: A resolution desk handling "Right to be Forgotten" or "Data Portability" requests.
- **Privacy by Design (`/privacy-by-design`)**: Safeguards mapped directly into your infrastructure planning.

## ⚖️ 5. Framework & Jurisdictional Compliance
- **Active Frameworks (`/frameworks`)**: Support for intersecting regulations (EU AI Act, DPDPA, ISO 42001, NIST).
- **Control Library (`/controls`)**: Centralized repository of security and ethical controls mapped across multiple standards.
- **Gap Analysis**: Real-time identification of missing evidence or policy violations.

## 🌍 6. Export & Trade (Vertical Suites)
*Tailored regulatory suites for Manufacturing MSMEs and Healthcare.*
- **Export Intelligence (`/export`)**: Real-time tracking of export documents mapped against destination country rules (US FDA, UKCA, CE).
- **Factory Ops (`/manufacturing`)**: Specialized trackers for Factory Licenses, GPCB/SPCB Consent, and Labor law requirements.
- **Global Bridge**: Translate your current posture to meet requirements in new international markets.

## 🔔 7. Incidents & Breaches
- **Incident Logging (`/incidents`)**: Managed workspace for logging system outages, AI hallucinations, or data breaches.
- **Breach Assessment**: Automated evaluation of breach severity to assist in regulatory reporting windows.

## 📁 8. Documents & Evidence
- **Document Vault (`/documents/vault`)**: Multi-modal ingestion of PDF, Images, and WhatsApp attachments with identifiable metadata.
- **Evidence Repository (`/evidence`)**: Automated API connectors pulling logs directly from GitHub, AWS, and ML platforms.
- **Auto-Generated Artifacts**: AI-powered drafting of compliance policies and technical documentation.

## 📊 9. Reporting & Audit
- **Automated Reports (`/reports/automated`)**: Scheduled generation of executive summaries and regulatory submission packages.
- **Audit Trails**: Non-repudiable logs of all compliance actions and changes within the cockpit.

## ⚙️ 10. Settings & Integrations
- **Organization Profile**: Centralized management of legal entities and operational regions.
- **Integrations**: Connect to your existing tech stack (Slack, Jira, AWS, GitHub) for automated evidence collection.

---

### Platform Architecture
- **Backend**: Python FastAPI with Asynchronous Redis + Celery job queuing.
- **Frontend**: Next.js (React 18) with clean enterprise SaaS design principles.
- **Security**: AES-256 encryption for evidence and strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).

*With aic Privacy Cockpit, compliance is a verifiable, automated engineering layer built right into the core of your business.*
